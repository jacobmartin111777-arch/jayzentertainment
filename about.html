About Jay'z Entertainment

We're here to get you on the track.

Jay'z Entertainment started from a simple idea: RC cars should be fast, fun, and easy to get into. Whether you're buying your first car or upgrading to something serious, we stock a range of vehicles and parts so you can race, crawl, or drift without the guesswork.

What We Do

We sell ready-to-run and kit RC cars, plus batteries, chargers, and spare parts. Everything we carry is something we'd run ourselves—so you get solid performance and reliability, not just a big catalog.

  • Fast shipping — Most orders ship within 1–2 business days.
  • Support when you need it — Questions? We're a quick email or call away.
